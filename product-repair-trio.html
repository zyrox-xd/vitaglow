The Repair Trio is a complete skincare system designed to address multiple skin concerns at once. It combines deep cleansing, gentle exfoliation, and intensive overnight repair into one powerful routine. Whether you're dealing with dark spots, uneven texture, dullness, or early signs of aging, this trio has you covered.


The Skin Whitening Soap starts your routine by purifying the skin and removing impurities. The Papaya Brightening Soap follows with gentle enzymatic exfoliation to reveal fresher, brighter skin. Finally, the Advanced Night Cream works overnight with retinol, hyaluronic acid, and niacinamide to repair damage, fade dark spots, and lock in moisture.

Advanced Night Cream

  • Retinol Complex – cell renewal
  • Hyaluronic Acid – intense moisture
  • Niacinamide – brightens & evens
  • Vitamin E – antioxidant protection

Skin Whitening Soap

  • Papaya Enzyme – gentle exfoliation
  • Kojic Acid – brightens & evens
  • Vitamin C – antioxidant protection
  • Aloe Vera – soothes & calms

Papaya Brightening Soap

  • Papain Enzyme – gentle exfoliation
  • Vitamin A – cell turnover
  • Lactic Acid – smooths & brightens
  • Aloe Vera – soothes & calms

Step 1 – Purify: Wet your face and lather the Skin Whitening Soap. Massage onto damp skin for 60 seconds. Rinse thoroughly.

Step 2 – Brighten & Exfoliate: Wet your face again and lather the Papaya Brightening Soap. Massage in circular motions for 60–90 seconds. Rinse thoroughly.

Step 3 – Repair: Pat your face dry. Apply a pearl‑sized amount of Advanced Night Cream to your face and neck. Massage upward until fully absorbed.


Pro tip: For best results, use this routine consistently every evening.
